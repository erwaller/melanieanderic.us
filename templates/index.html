Directions

From Manhattan

  1. Head southeast on The Brooklyn Bridge.
  2. Take the exit toward Bklyn-Qns Expy.
  3. Merge onto Cadman Plaza W/Old Fulton St.
  4. Continue onto Columbia St.
  5. Turn right onto Sackett St.
  6. Take the 1st left onto Van Brunt St.
  7. Turn right onto Beard St.
  8. Entrance will be straight ahead.

From Brooklyn Battery Tunnel

  1. Take exit 26 to merge onto Hamilton Ave.
  2. Turn right onto Clinton St.
  3. Go 4 blocks and turn right onto Lorraine St.
  4. Go 5 blocks and turn left onto Dwight St.
  5. Go 4 blocks and turn right onto Beard St.
  6. Keep going Straight Ahead.

From Brooklyn or Long Island

  1. Take Belt Parkway towards Manhattan to Hamilton Avenue (“Last Exit in Brooklyn”).
  2. Go straight at exit ramp and make a left on Van Brunt Street.
  3. Follow directions at top of page.

From Long Island Expressway

  1. Take to Brooklyn Queens Expressway West towards Atlantic Avenue Exit.
  2. At light, make left onto Columbia Street.
  3. Follow directions at top of page.
