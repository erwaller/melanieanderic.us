Our Story

It's pretty hard for us to pin down exactly when we met, but we can both agree on our first memory of becoming friends. We were chosen to play in the very prestigous string quartet at Lakeside Elementary School. During our big preformance, Melanie hit a wrong note and cried in frustration. Eric continued playing and made it through the performance. It was the first time we worked on anything together and although it was a rocky start, we became, and stayed friends.

Soon enough we were in high school, Eric had lost his baby fat, Melanie had a full set of braces, and the sparks flew! In 2003, Eric invited Melanie to watch his band play (yea Kingston12!) and she gleefully accepted. During the set Eric hooked his bass guitar up to a wireless amp, strolled over to Melanie and serenaded her. It was a little out of character for Eric to do something so outgoing, but it did the trick, and Melanie was smitten.

We spent high school side by and side, and then in college, we decided on separate schools, cities, and states. But after those 5 years were over we decided enough was enough and we moved in together in NYC. Finally, after being friends for almost 16 years, dating for 10, and living together for 3, Eric popped the question on a beautiful rooftop in our NYC neighborhood. Surrounded by some of our amazing friends and family, we decided to make it offical!

Directions

Our wedding will take place in Brooklyn, NY. We're so excited to have our wedding in the city that we call home, but we know many of you will be traveling to be with us, so we've planned some accomodations!

We recommend you stay in the New York Marriott at the Brooklyn Bridge. You can reserve a room by calling 1-718-246-7000. We've reserved a room block for our wedding guests, so just remember to say "Melanie and Eric" when you call!

  2. Take the exit toward Bklyn-Qns Expy.

  4. Continue onto Columbia St.

  7. Turn right onto Beard St.
  8. Entrance will be straight ahead.

  4. Go 5 blocks and turn left onto Dwight St.

From Brooklyn or Long Island

  3. Follow directions at top of page.
